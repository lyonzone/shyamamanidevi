AWARDS, SAMMANS AND TITLES CONFERRED & BESTOWED UPON

As a recognition to her contributions in the development, enrichment and popularizing “Odishi” music in the State and National level, Dr. (Mrs) Shyamamani Devi has been conferred the honorary Doctorate degree (Ph.D) by Utkal University of Culture, Bhubaneswar during 2012. The documentary film on the life and achievement of Dr Shyamamani Devi prepared by Film Division, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India was released on 8th October 2017 at New Delhi by honorable Union Cabinet Minister of Ministry of Tribal Affairs,Shri Jual Oram with Shri Arun Kumar Panda,IAS, Secretary to the Government of India.

Besides, she has also been awarded and conferred over 140 awards, 12 titles& a good number of sammans (honours) & felicitations by Central & State Governments, Public and Private Institutions &various Clubs of Odisha, other States as well as Overseas.
